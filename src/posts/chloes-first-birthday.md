<p style="text-align: center;">Meet Chloe!  She was the winner of my first baby photo contest on facebook.  There were over 50 entries and 500 votes.  Chloe was the adorable winner.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Attached is the picture that she won with.  <a href="http://www.facebook.com/photo.php?pid=38674872&amp;o=all&amp;op=1&amp;view=all&amp;subj=88773417960&amp;aid=-1&amp;id=34304858" target="_blank">http://www.facebook.com/photo.php?pid=38674872&amp;o=all&amp;op=1&amp;view=all&amp;subj=88773417960&amp;aid=-1&amp;id=34304858</a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">She won a free sitting and 11&#215;14 wall art print!</p>

<p style="text-align: center;">Chloe has the biggest personality!  She was all about taking pictures and for a one year old that&apos;s not usually the case.  She was all smiles and good times.  I think the following pictures show Chloe at her best.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-424" title="chloe7" src="/media/chloe7.jpg" alt="" width="601" height="400" srcset="/media/chloe7.jpg 601w, /media/chloe7-300x200.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 601px) 100vw, 601px" /></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">This is her favorite little piano, and stuffed bunny.  I&apos;m not a big prop person but when the objects are meaningful I think it makes for a great picture.  Don&apos;t ever hesitate to bring your childs favorite blanket, stuffed animal, or toy.  Those objects that your child endears so mucy will soon be gone and all you&apos;ll have to remember is a picture.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-429" title="chloe2" src="/media/chloe2.jpg" alt="" width="600" height="400" srcset="/media/chloe2.jpg 600w, /media/chloe2-300x200.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" /></p>
