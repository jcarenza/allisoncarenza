<p style="text-align: center;">One of the perks of my job is creating the wedding album for the bride and groom.  A family heirloom.  Maybe even something on the &#8220;to grab&#8221; list in the case of a fire.  Yes, I do love the albums.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">I just recently made a switch in album makers.  I now use <a href="http://http://www.unitedalbums.com/">United Albums</a>, Maggie recommended them, and I won&apos;t be turning back any time soon.  The customer service is great, and they make sure I&apos;m happy happy happy!  And, I am.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-3530" title="album5" src="/media/album5-681x1024.jpg" alt="" width="681" height="1024" srcset="/media/album5-681x1024.jpg 681w, /media/album5-200x300.jpg 200w, /media/album5-768x1154.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 681px) 100vw, 681px" /></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Last year I turned over the album designing to Elizabeth Lauber from <a href="http://http://www.elizamariedesigns.com/">Eliza Marie Designs</a>.  I could go on and on about how great she is, but I&apos;ll just give you a few reasons.  She&apos;s fast, she&apos;s friendly, and she&apos;s a true artist.  And, she&apos;s really gotten to know my style.  The album she just designed needed no changes at all!  She nailed it.  Love her!</p>
