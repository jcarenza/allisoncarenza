<p style="text-align: center;">I&apos;m really hard on my work.  I pick it apart.  I expect excellence from myself.  But, when the wedding album arrives on my doorstep all that goes away and I just love the look of my work!  It&apos;s like a shiny present that I don&apos;t get to keep.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">
<p style="text-align: center;">I get my albums printed at <a href="http://http://www.unitedalbums.com/">United Albums</a>, if you decide to give them a try tell Dorothy I said hi!  She&apos;s hilarious and loves to take care of me.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">And, while I love getting the album, I don&apos;t love designing it, so I outsource all those hours to Elizabeth Lauber.  You can see her work<a href="http://http://www.elizamariedesigns.com/"> here</a>.  She&apos;s super fast, she&apos;s oh so easy to work with, and she really makes an effort to learn my style.  In fact, after working together for a year there were absolutely no changes that needed to be made to the album.  She ROCKS!</p>
