<p style="text-align: center;">Getting to watch kids grow up in front of my camera is one of the great joys of my job.   I&#8217;ve known little Colton since before he was born, and now he is turning ONE!   I can always count on Colton for one thing&#8230;to be cute!  He always shares his adorable smile with me.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3123" title="yoder5" src="/media/yoder5.jpg" alt="" width="700" height="499" srcset="/media/yoder5.jpg 700w, /media/yoder5-300x214.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 700px) 100vw, 700px" /></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">His sister Claire was loving the balloons I brought.  Especially the pink polka dot balloon that matched her outfit.  That is, until it flew into a tree.  Not even 3 yet and talking in complete sentences, it&#8217;s easy to forget she&#8217;s still a 2 year old.  She wouldn&#8217;t stop talking about the pink balloon even though I had others.  She would cry for a second, and then move on, but not for long.  Eventually she would remember.  So when her mom Amy explained that now the birds could enjoy the pink balloon.  It should come as no surprise to me that her response would be, &#8220;Have the birds bring it back down&#8221;.  Wow!  What a smart girl!</p>
