<p style="text-align: center;">Smizing!  It&apos;s alway my goal.  To get the smile to travel all the way up to the eyes.  Not an easy feat with kids that have been saying &#8220;cheese&#8221; for years, but I try my hardest to find that smile in the eyes.  In fact, I don&apos;t settle for less.  When I&apos;m looking through the camera I&apos;m waiting for that moment.  The real one, where the subject feels connected to me, to the camera, and to the moment.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4259" title="johnson1" src="/media/johnson1.jpg" alt="" width="930" height="680"   /></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">And then there are real moments when I&apos;m taking pictures like this one below.  There is nothing I could have said or done to get them to do this so perfectly.</p>
