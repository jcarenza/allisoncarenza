<p style="text-align: center;">Smizing!  It&#8217;s alway my goal.  To get the smile to travel all the way up to the eyes.  Not an easy feat with kids that have been saying &#8220;cheese&#8221; for years, but I try my hardest to find that smile in the eyes.  In fact, I don&#8217;t settle for less.  When I&#8217;m looking through the camera I&#8217;m waiting for that moment.  The real one, where the subject feels connected to me, to the camera, and to the moment.</p>
