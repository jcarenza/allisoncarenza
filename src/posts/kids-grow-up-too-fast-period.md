<p style="text-align: center;">It&#8217;s hard to believe 4 years ago I took pictures of little Izzie just days after she was born.  It&#8217;s scary really.  Since then I&#8217;ve done countless shoots of these two and trust me it never gets old.  Their mom Kim is a wonderful client that has become a good friend.  It&#8217;s nice to have the same clients come back year after year.  I get to develop a real relationship with the kids and the parents.  And I love watching them grow and change.</p>
