<p style="text-align: center;">So I haven&apos;t even begun to download the hundreds of pictures I took at the Bat Mitzvah on Saturday.  I did however have the red carpet pictures downloaded.  Let me explain...</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">This Bat Mitzvah was for Melanie, the adorable brunette third from the right.  She wanted her party to be movie star quality complete with a red carpet entrance, paparazzi, and Taylor Lautner.  I&apos;m pretty sure the following picture isn&apos;t that far from reality for poor Taylor.</p>
