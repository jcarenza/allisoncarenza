<p style="text-align: center;">I remember I was walking around Costco the first time Brooke brought up Justin&#8217;s name in conversation.  She said it casually, &#8220;I met a boy&#8221;.  I jumped at the opportunity to hear more because, well Brooke is always guarded with her heart, when it comes to men.  &#8220;What? Tell me more.&#8221;  As though my enthusiasm was a reminder of her heart she quickly brushed it off as no big deal.  I didn&#8217;t stop though, I forced her to tell me how they met, what he was like, and how long this had been going on.  She made it seem simple, no big deal, but I could hear her smiling through the phone.  I knew this boy had broken down a wall.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">So when I met Justin for the first time, it was no surprise Brooke was glowing.  He constantly showered her with attention and affection.  She was being treated like a queen, as she should be.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">A few years later, and many memories it&#8217;s their wedding day.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">I was the lucky photographer, and because Brooke and Justin are perfection, and the location was amazing, this wedding was a photographer&#8217;s dream.  Here&#8217;s the story of their wedding day.</p>

<p style="text-align: center;">On the right is David, the owner of the plantation,  a fine southern gentleman, and as refined as the genuine silver you see below.  He&#8217;s all about following the rules of etiquette,  anything less is not  suitable for his house.  He was also Brooke&#8217;s wedding coordinator, and one of my favorite people at the wedding.  When I left, I thanked him for a great day, and he told me I had met the wildest man in the south.  I don&#8217;t doubt that for a second.</p>
