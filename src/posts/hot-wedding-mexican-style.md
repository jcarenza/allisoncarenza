<p style="text-align: center;">With 12 of their closest friends there to witness, and the warm sea breeze at their backs, Cara and Ian tied the knot.  They decided the ONLY way to get married was on the beach.  After watching the wedding I think they were entirely right.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">It&#8217;s really hard for me to put into words the love I witnesses on this sunny June day.  I will try to show you, but even these beautiful pics can&#8217;t do their wedding justice.</p>
