<p style="text-align: center;">
<p style="text-align: center;">Some photos just make my day.  I literally sit at my computer editing with a goofy grin on my face.  With the Roling kids that seems to be the case every time.  This session was suppose to be for baby Alex&#8217;s first birthday, but as always Eden jumped in and stole the show with her charm, her curls, and her big blue eyes.</p>

<p style="text-align: center;">That&#8217;s not to say Alex didn&#8217;t have his moments.  He&#8217;s just a little more shy, a little more reserved than big sister.  His charm is quiet and adorable.</p>

<p style="text-align: center;">There&#8217;s always a sense of sadness when I finish a year of shoots with a family.  I have gotten to see the little one grow up right in front of my camera, I&#8217;ve gotten close to the siblings and even more close to the parents.  I think Danielle is awesome.  I&#8217;ve loved every second, so knowing I&#8217;m not going to get to see them again for 6 months is disappointing.  But I&#8217;ll manage.  Already looking forward to our next shoot!</p>
