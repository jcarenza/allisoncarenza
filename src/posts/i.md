<p style="text-align: center;">
<p style="text-align: center;">Meet Desiree, and David.  Two cops having a baby.  Sounds like a sitcom, but their relationship is anything but a funny daytime TV show.</p>
<p><a rel="attachment wp-att-1758" href="http://www.allisoncarenza.com/archives/1739/des4-2"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1758" title="des4" src="http://www.allisoncarenza.com/studio/wp-content/uploads/2010/11/des41.jpg" alt="" width="742" height="500" srcset="/media/des41.jpg 742w, /media/des41-300x202.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 742px) 100vw, 742px" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Desiree is the type of person that makes everyone feel important.  During her maternity shoot she was brave, confident, and willing to be silly with me.</p>
<p><a rel="attachment wp-att-1757" href="http://www.allisoncarenza.com/archives/1739/des3-2"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1757" title="des3" src="http://www.allisoncarenza.com/studio/wp-content/uploads/2010/11/des31.jpg" alt="" width="693" height="500" srcset="/media/des31.jpg 693w, /media/des31-300x216.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 693px) 100vw, 693px" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">David did everything he could to make Desiree feel beautiful.  During maternity shoots it&apos;s not really about the boys, their job is to admire their glowing wife.  I would say David was pretty obsessed with Desiree during the shoot.</p>
