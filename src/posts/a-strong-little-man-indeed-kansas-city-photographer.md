<p style="text-align: center;">This is little Ellis!   And he is one of the most perfect 3 month olds...ever.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2905" title="ellis1" src="/media/ellis1.jpg" alt="" width="693" height="500" srcset="/media/ellis1.jpg 693w, /media/ellis1-300x216.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 693px) 100vw, 693px" /></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">His parents Andrea and Roman are rockstarts, and I got to take their pics when Ellis was still a bun in the oven.  We had plans to do newborn pics, but when Ellis was born his legs had to be immediately casted.  Ellis had a <a href="http://www.mayoclinic.com/health/clubfoot/DS00814">club foot</a>.  He was a tough cookie just like his parents and after a few months of casts he is doing great.  He still has to wear cute little boots until he&apos;s 5 months old.  It&apos;s not easy for any of them, but their love for each other flows freely.  In fact Ellis&apos;s grandma was at the shoot.  When I asked her what it felt to be a grandma for the first time, without hesitation she told me it was liquid love flowing through your heart.  I&apos;ll never forget those words.  Or her warm smile when she said it.</p>
