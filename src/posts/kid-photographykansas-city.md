<p style="text-align: center;">Sometimes kids come into the studio timid, fearful, and downright scared.  Ok I take that back, most of the time kids, that are new, come in that way.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">I mean really I&apos;d be scared too.  Imagine from their point of view, a big dark room, a big scary box things that shoots light, sitting in the middle of a room while  a crazy lady aims a big black thing at you.   All the while your parents are in the background acting crazy and telling you to smile.  I&apos;d be intimidated too.   Baby girl Brooksley was no different.  She was attached to her mom Beth like a leech.  So we just played.  And after a few minutes here are the shots we started getting. <a rel="attachment wp-att-956" href="http://www.allisoncarenza.com/archives/952/b4"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-956" title="b4" src="/media/b4.jpg" alt="" width="600" height="600" srcset="/media/b4.jpg 600w, /media/b4-150x150.jpg 150w, /media/b4-300x300.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" /></a></p>
<p><a rel="attachment wp-att-957" href="http://www.allisoncarenza.com/archives/952/b5"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-957" title="b5" src="/media/b5.jpg" alt="" width="600" height="400" srcset="/media/b5.jpg 600w, /media/b5-300x200.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Brooksley has beautiful red hair and big brown eyes as you can see.  The camera loves her!  And after a short time, she was starting to love the camera.  Just look.</p>
<p><a rel="attachment wp-att-958" href="http://www.allisoncarenza.com/archives/952/b6"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-958" title="b6" src="/media/b6.jpg" alt="" width="601" height="400" srcset="/media/b6.jpg 601w, /media/b6-300x200.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 601px) 100vw, 601px" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">She even stuck her tongue out.  I think we told her to make a funny face.  Job well done Brooksley! <a rel="attachment wp-att-955" href="http://www.allisoncarenza.com/archives/952/b3"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-955" title="b3" src="/media/b3.jpg" alt="" width="601" height="400" srcset="/media/b3.jpg 601w, /media/b3-300x200.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 601px) 100vw, 601px" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">The picture below is something powerful to me.  I look at it as a baby no longer a baby.  Ready to get rid of the Bumbo and stand on her own.  Makes me take a deep breath and give thanks that I captured this awesome moment.<a rel="attachment wp-att-959" href="http://www.allisoncarenza.com/archives/952/b7"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-959" title="b7" src="/media/b7.jpg" alt="" width="399" height="600" srcset="/media/b7.jpg 399w, /media/b7-200x300.jpg 200w" sizes="(max-width: 399px) 100vw, 399px" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Brooksley you are beautiful!  Thanks for making your pictures so easy to take!</p>
