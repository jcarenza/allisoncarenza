<p style="text-align: center;">I&apos;ve known Alice since college, in fact I was one of the first people she met on campus as her Freshman orientation assistant.  Unlike many freshman Alice was confident, outgoing, and oh so happy, she wasn&apos;t afraid to show it and share it.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">If I had to pick one word for Alice it would be passionate.  She had passion for the people in her life, her religion, and her theatre practice.  It should come as no surprise that she was a rock star on stage.  Years later I&apos;m happy to report Alice is still sharing that passion and zest for life.  In fact, she is even more in love with life now that she has  two beautiful babies!  I had the honor of capturing some tender moments with her kids on a cold December day.</p>
