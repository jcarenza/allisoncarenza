<p style="text-align: center;">Prepare yourself!  It&apos;s the Bondi boys, and they are even more adorable in person than they are in these pics!</p>

<p><a rel="attachment wp-att-1098" href="http://www.allisoncarenza.com/archives/1090/bon8"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1098" title="bon8" src="http:/media/bon8.jpg" alt="" width="400" height="600" srcset="/media/bon8.jpg 400w, /media/bon8-200x300.jpg 200w" sizes="(max-width: 400px) 100vw, 400px" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Michael decided he should hang from a tree.  Check out those awesome teeth.  He did this when the camera wasn&apos;t ready, so I had him redo it, and he did.  Just like that.  Sometimes it&apos;s moments like this when you realize how fast time flies.  I remember taking Michael&apos;s pics when he wasn&apos;t  walking, now he&apos;s hanging form trees.</p>

<p style="text-align: center;">This is one of my favs from the shoot.  I knew the rocks would be the perfect spot, but the light was all patchy coming through the trees, so I snapped a few off just because.  Just as I did the clouds handed me a gift by covering up the sun and acting as a softbox.  I clicked a few more.  As it turns out I like the patchy light ones better.  Sometimes as a photographer you just never know.</p>
