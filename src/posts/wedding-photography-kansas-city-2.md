<p style="text-align: center;">This love started with a boy and a girl.  A boy not sure of marriage, and a girl sure of love.  And sure enough they found each other!</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">It wasn&apos;t coincidence, it wasn&apos;t by chance, it wasn&apos;t a random meeting at a bar, this relationship was fate.  Let me explain.  Amber is a teacher, and at parent teacher conferences one of her parents, Cheryl, tried to hook her up with her handsome cousin Sean.  Amber was all no way, so of course she didn&apos;t pursue.  Then a year later while coaching cross country, Cheryl again tried to convince Amber that dating her cousin was the best idea ever.  Amber must have given in because Sean called her and they had a fateful first date.  I guess the rest is history because after 5 years of fun, they are now married.</p>
