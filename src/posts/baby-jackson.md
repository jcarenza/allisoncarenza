<p><a rel="attachment wp-att-461" href="http://www.allisoncarenza.com/studio/archives/459/lager3"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-461" title="Lager3" src="http://www.allisoncarenza.com/studio/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/Lager3.jpg" alt="" width="400" height="600" srcset="/media/Lager3.jpg 400w, /media/Lager3-200x300.jpg 200w" sizes="(max-width: 400px) 100vw, 400px" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">
<p style="text-align: center;">I often look at other artists work for ideas and the babies are sleeping, I just don&apos;t have that experience in my studio.  Most of the time my clients are bright eyed and bushy tailed just like the above photo.  Jackson was alert and looking right at the camera which made for some great shots.  The below shot just shows how tiny his little hands are compared to his dad&apos;s.</p>
