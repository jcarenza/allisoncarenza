<p style="text-align: center;">Meet Ainsley the proud baby of parents Jenni, and Joel.  Her parents have hard core baby love syndrome.  They are the definition of obsessed.  I don&apos;t think I need to say much about these newborn pictures, because the photos do their emotions justice.</p>
<p><a rel="attachment wp-att-1388" href="http://www.allisoncarenza.com/archives/1385/joel3"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1388" title="joel3" src="/media/joel3.jpg" alt="" width="650" height="480" srcset="/media/joel3.jpg 650w, /media/joel3-300x222.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 650px) 100vw, 650px" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">I do want to say that Jenni is a gorgeous momma.  Red hair is my absolute favorite to photograph.  Perfection!</p>
<p><a rel="attachment wp-att-1390" href="http://www.allisoncarenza.com/archives/1385/joel5"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1390" title="joel5" src="/media/joel5.jpg" alt="" width="751" height="500" srcset="/media/joel5.jpg 751w, /media/joel5-300x200.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 751px) 100vw, 751px" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Funny story, Joel and I swam on the same swim team as kids.  He didn&apos;t know I was Allison McIntosh until he got to my house and we started taking pictures. Halfway through the shoot he made the connection and I remembered.  Pretty funny how things work out.  Joel was quite the swimmer as a kid now he&apos;s quite the daddy.</p>
