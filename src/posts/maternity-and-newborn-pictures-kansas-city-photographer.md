<p style="text-align: center;">It was her laugh that I noticed first.  During our shoot Tish just had a way of making me smile when she laughed.  Not because it was big, or obnoxious, or silly, but it was real, and I could tell.  That&#8217;s what I noticed first, then I noticed how Jermaine always noticed.  He smiled too.</p>
<p><a rel="attachment wp-att-1933" href="http://www.allisoncarenza.com/archives/1930/ti5"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1933" title="ti5" src="http://www.allisoncarenza.com/studio/wp-content/uploads/2011/01/ti5.jpg" alt="" width="700" height="499" srcset="/media/ti5.jpg 700w, /media/ti5-300x214.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 700px) 100vw, 700px" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Maybe the glowing came from the  fact that this pregnancy was a true blessing for them, or, maybe they just glow when they&#8217;re together.  They tried for a long time before realizing they needed help.  This story is unfortunately all too common in my studio, but I love all the success stories I get to witness.  In those precious moments when clients expose their hearts and hardships to me I am humbled.  But to Jermaine it&#8217;s just a miracle to get pregnant at all.</p>
<p><a rel="attachment wp-att-1932" href="http://www.allisoncarenza.com/archives/1930/ti1"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1932" title="ti1" src="http://www.allisoncarenza.com/studio/wp-content/uploads/2011/01/ti1.jpg" alt="" width="700" height="499" srcset="/media/ti1.jpg 700w, /media/ti1-300x214.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 700px) 100vw, 700px" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Then came baby!</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">A BIG BABY!!!!!  So big in fact Tish had to have a c-section.   Beautiful, perfect  Malachi was born into the arms of two very excited and very loving parents.  Just a few days later I got to meet him.  For an hour and a half we admired the ahh of a newborn baby.  It never really gets old.</p>
