<p style="text-align: center;">The plan was to do maternity pics first, but that plan was busted when baby Michael Vernon arrived early.  Donna was disappointed, but I&#8217;m sure a little relieved to have the pregnancy over.  By the time I see women, they are over being pregnant.  Let me back up, Donna started her family later in life.  Her first daughter Alexis was a perfect accident. But a few years later  when she wanted to try to have another child she couldn&#8217;t.  By that time she was entering her 40&#8217;s, and doctors were discouraging.  She didn&#8217;t listen and it&#8217;s a good thing because now she&#8217;s got a beautiful boy!  Meet Michael!</p>

<p style="text-align: center;">Michael is the perfect newborn to photograph.  He didn&#8217;t need to eat all the time, he didn&#8217;t cry much, and all he really wanted to do was sleep!</p>
