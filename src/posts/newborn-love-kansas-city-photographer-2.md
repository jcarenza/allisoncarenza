<p style="text-align: center;">
<p style="text-align: center;">I could go on and on about how much I love my job, but one of the best parts of being a newborn photographer is the quiet moments when the baby is asleep, nuzzled in the warm blanket, and all I hear is the little voice in my heart reminding me of how lucky I am to be in that moment.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3741" title="talm1" src="/media/talm1.jpg" alt="" width="930" height="680" srcset="/media/talm1.jpg 930w, /media/talm1-300x219.jpg 300w, /media/talm1-768x562.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 930px) 100vw, 930px" /></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">And then there are the loud moments, when brothers erupt in laughter, and I have my camera ready.  I wish I could say this was shared laughter, but this was an inside joke between brothers.  They&apos;re probably laughing at me.</p>
